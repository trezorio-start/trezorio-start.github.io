FAQs

Q: What is Trezor?‍

Trezor is a hardware wallet designed to securely store your cryptocurrencies offline, away from potential cyber threats.

Q: How does Trezor work?

‍Trezor stores your private keys offline, ensuring that your funds are protected from hacking attempts and malware.

Q: Is Trezor safe?

‍Yes, Trezor is renowned for its security features, including PIN protection, passphrase encryption, and two-factor authentication.

Q: Can I use Trezor with multiple cryptocurrencies?

‍Yes, Trezor supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many more.

Q: How do I set up my Trezor device?

‍Setting up your Trezor device is easy. Simply visit trezor.io/start and follow the on-screen instructions to get started.

Q: What is a seed phrase?

‍A seed phrase is a series of words generated during the setup process that acts as a backup for your private keys. It's essential to keep your seed phrase secure and never share it with anyone.

Q: Can I recover my funds if I lose my Trezor device?

‍ Yes, as long as you have your seed phrase, you can recover your funds on a new Trezor device or compatible wallet.

Q: How can I update my Trezor firmware?

‍You can update your Trezor firmware using the Trezor Wallet interface or the Trezor Bridge software on your computer.

Q: Can I use Trezor with mobile devices?

‍Yes, you can connect your Trezor device to compatible mobile wallets using an OTG cable or Bluetooth connection.

Q: Is customer support available for Trezor users?

‍Yes, Trezor offers dedicated customer support to assist users with any questions or issues they may encounter.
